About Me

The most exciting parts of working with a small-sized company is launching a new product. At my first internship, I wrote transformers for our models or explanations for our diagrams throughout my college career, whether it was between my classes or late into the night. In our team meetings, we dreamed about our pipeline with continuous delivery, and how they will lead to brand name recognition and gain the interest of more potential customers. Once, after a hard day of work, my manager walked in with a smile from one ear to another and announced our pipeline is ready, and we are continuously updating the models. I was surprised and even more excited; all those obstacles that felt overwhelming were finally overcome. We were giving high fives to each other and recognizing that each of us were instrumental to release the product. I live for these moments and hope that I can continue to experience different product releases.
